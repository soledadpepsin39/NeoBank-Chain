## 🎯 Our Mission

NeoBank Chain was born from a simple but powerful observation:
**the traditional banking system has failed billions of people.**

Too many fees. Too many intermediaries. Too many barriers.
Too little transparency. Too little freedom. Too little control.

At the same time, **DeFi (Decentralized Finance)** has created incredible innovation —
but remains too complex, too risky, and too intimidating for most people.

**The world needs a bridge. NeoBank Chain is that bridge.**

## 🔑 Core Principles

**1. Total Control**
Every user is the sole owner of their funds — in both CeFi and DeFi.
Choose how much control you want: fully custodial, semi-custodial, or fully self-custodial.

**2. Universal Accessibility**
NeoBank Chain works for everyone:
- The grandmother in Morocco who just wants a simple bank account
- The entrepreneur in Nigeria sending international payments
- The developer in France exploring DeFi yield strategies
- The investor in Asia managing a diversified crypto portfolio

**3. Radical Transparency**
Every blockchain transaction is publicly verifiable on Solana.
Every CeFi fee is clearly displayed — no hidden charges. Ever.

NeoBank Chain is designed to be compliant in every market it enters —
making it the first DeFi-integrated product that institutions can trust.
